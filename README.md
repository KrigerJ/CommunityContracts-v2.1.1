Community Contracts v2.1.1

Turn Stardew Valley into a thriving cooperative economy by partnering with townspeople through contracts and on‑demand services. Version 2.1.1 introduces a streamlined toolbar system, major new farming and automation services, expanded language support, and significant improvements to harvesting and crab pot workflows.

What’s New in v2.1.1

Unified Toolbar System
- A single hotkey now opens the Service Toolbar, giving access to all features.
- No more multiple hotkeys — everything is organized into a clean, intuitive menu.
-Set the Hotkey either using GMCM or in the config.json file.

New Languages
- Added Korean and Italian, bringing total supported languages to 9.
- 
New Character Services

Each service:
- Uses items from your backpack first when consumables are needed
- Charges a small fee per action
- Grants Friendship Points with the character performing the work
- Works across all locations, including sheds, barns, coops, and outdoor maps
- 
A) Farming Services

1. Planting Service
Hire a character to plant seeds from your backpack in:
- Garden pots
- Tilled soil
- Any valid location across the valley
- 
2. Tilling Service
Characters can till a custom rectangular area you define.
They will also:
- Water the newly tilled soil
- Auto‑plant any valid seeds from your backpack (correct season + valid tile)
- 
3. Watering Service
Characters water:
- All dry garden pots
- All dry tilled soil
Across every location you own.

4. Garden Pot Setting Service
Originally designed to turn sheds into mini‑greenhouses:
- Characters place garden pots inside your adjustable rectangular zone
- Uses pots from your inventory first, then charges a purchase fee
- Works in most indoor spaces
- Pots may block paths — remove manually if needed
- Fully compatible with planting, watering, and harvesting services
- 
5. Bee House Setting Service
Characters place bee houses in your adjustable rectangular zone:
- Works outdoors, including hard‑to‑reach areas
- Uses your inventory first, then charges a purchase fee for bee houses
- 
6. Honey Collecting + Optional Mead Processing
Characters collect honey from all bee houses:
- Honey quality scales with the character’s level
- Optional: automatically convert honey → mead before delivery
- Mead quality matches honey quality
- Works even for bee houses placed in unreachable spots
- 
7. Animal Care Service
Characters:
- Feed farm animals by filling troughs
- Pet farm animals indoors and outdoors
- 
8. Seed Making Service
Characters convert crops in your backpack into seed packets:
- Higher quality crops yield more seeds
- Perfect for seeds not currently sold in stores
- 
B) Other Services

1. Sashimi Service
Characters convert any fish in your backpack into sashimi:
- Sashimi quality matches fish quality
- Quantity scales with fish value
- Always profitable when sashimi is sold
- 
2. Ore Processing Service
Characters smelt your ore into metal bars:
- Uses 5 ore per bar from your backpack.
- 
3. Tapper Setting Service
Characters install tappers on all tappable trees within your rectangular zone:
- Uses your tappers first
- Automatically purchases more if needed for a fee
- 
Improved Services

Harvesting Service Fixes:
Crop harvesting now works reliably across all locations

Crab Pot Setting Improvements:
- No longer places pots on bridges or blocking paths
- Pots are automatically baited when set
- Uses your bait first, then charges a bait purchase fee
- 
Crab Pot Harvesting Improvements:
- Pots are automatically re‑baited after harvesting
- Uses your bait first, then charges a purchase fee

- Adjustable Rectangular Service Area
Used for:
- Tilling
- Garden pot placement
- Bee house placement
- Tapper Placement
-Set width and length via the new Area Adjustment Toolbar.

Existing Features from v2.1.0 (Still Included)

- Multi‑language support (now expanded to 9 languages)
- Crab pot services (setting, baiting, collecting)
- Tapper product collection
- Processor Time Reduction (optional)
- Configurable parameters via GMCM
- Delivery chest system with customizable colors and labels
- Contract growth tied to friendship and skill
- Specialist and non‑specialist NPC roles
  
Summary
Community Contracts v2.1.1 transforms Stardew Valley into a dynamic cooperative economy where townspeople become your partners in farming, crafting, gathering, and automation. With a unified toolbar, expanded service offerings, improved harvesting and crab pot logic, and broader language support, this update makes the entire system smoother, smarter, and more immersive than ever.
